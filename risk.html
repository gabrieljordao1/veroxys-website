Risk Disclosure

Last updated: May 26, 2025

1. Nature of Futures Trading

Micro E-Mini Nasdaq 100 (MNQ) futures are leveraged instruments. Leverage can amplify both gains and losses, potentially exceeding your initial margin deposit.

2. Key Risks

  • Leverage Risk: Small adverse price movements can result in significant losses.
  • Margin Calls: You may need to deposit additional funds on short notice.
  • Slippage & Gapping: Volatile markets can cause trade executions at worse prices.
  • Liquidity Risk: In extreme conditions, you may not be able to exit positions promptly.
  • Counterparty Risk: Exchanges or clearinghouses may impose trading halts or restrictions.

3. Backtest Limitations

Historical simulations (e.g. 541.47% annualized returns) are based on past data and do not account for real-time slippage, commissions, or changing market structure. Past performance is not indicative of future results.

4. User Responsibilities

  • Setting appropriate position sizes and risk parameters.
  • Monitoring open trades and margin requirements continuously.
  • Ensuring your platform and internet connectivity remain stable.

5. No Guarantee of Profit

Veroxys makes no assurances of profitability. Trading carries inherent risks and you should only trade with capital you can afford to lose.

This disclosure is for informational purposes and not financial advice. Consult qualified professionals before making trading decisions.
