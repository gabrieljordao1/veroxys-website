High Risk Investment Warning

Trading futures, forex, and other leveraged products carries a high level of risk and may not be suitable for all investors. The high degree of leverage can work against you as well as for you. Before deciding to trade, you should carefully consider your investment objectives, level of experience, and risk appetite.

Risk of Loss

You should be aware of all the risks associated with trading and seek advice from an independent financial advisor if you have any doubts.

Specific Risks Include:

1. Market Risk

  • Markets can move against your positions rapidly
  • Sudden price movements can result in significant losses
  • Gap risk during market closures or low liquidity periods
  • Volatility can exceed historical norms without warning

2. Leverage Risk

  • Futures trading involves high leverage (often 10:1 or higher)
  • Small market movements can result in large losses
  • You may lose more than your initial investment
  • Margin calls may require additional funds immediately

3. Technology Risk

  • Software failures or bugs may cause unexpected losses
  • Internet connectivity issues can prevent trade execution
  • Computer hardware failures may interrupt trading
  • Broker platform outages can impact performance

4. Algorithmic Trading Risks

  • Past performance does not guarantee future results
  • Market conditions change and strategies may become ineffective
  • Over-optimization can lead to poor real-world performance
  • Execution slippage can significantly impact results
