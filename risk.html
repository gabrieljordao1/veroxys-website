Futures Trading Risk

Trading MNQ futures involves significant risks:

  • Leverage: Small moves can wipe out capital.
  • Margin Calls: You may need to deposit more funds.
  • Slippage: Prices may differ in fast markets.
  • Liquidity: Hard to exit in extreme volatility.

Backtest Limitations

Past results (e.g. 541.47% annual) are not guarantees of future performance.

User Responsibility

  • You set parameters, position size and manage risk.
  • You monitor open trades and margin requirements.

No Guarantees

Veroxys does not guarantee profit—past performance ≠ future results.
