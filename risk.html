Futures Trading Risk

Trading Micro E-Mini Nasdaq 100 (MNQ) futures is highly speculative and involves substantial risk:

  • Leverage Risk: Small price moves can lead to large gains or losses.
  • Margin Calls: You may be required to deposit additional funds.
  • Slippage: Orders may execute at different prices in fast markets.
  • Liquidity Risk: In extreme volatility, it may be difficult to exit positions.
