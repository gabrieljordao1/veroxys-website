About Veroxys

Founded by PhD-level quants, former hedge-fund traders and veteran software engineers, Veroxys brings over 50 years of combined expertise to algorithmic futures trading.

Our lead dev built alpha-generating strategies at a top New York hedge fund; our CTO architected low-latency systems for global firms.

We fuse rigorous research—published in the Journal of Financial Markets—with production-grade code and sub-millisecond connectivity.

At Veroxys, we're traders first—our mission is to democratize institutional-grade algorithms with transparency, robust risk controls, and best-in-class support.

Our Story

Veroxys was born from a simple observation: the tools and strategies that generate consistent returns in institutional trading were inaccessible to retail traders. Our founders, having spent decades in quantitative finance and high-frequency trading, decided to bridge this gap.

What started as an internal project to automate our personal trading evolved into a comprehensive suite of Expert Advisors. We realized that by combining our expertise in mathematical modeling, software engineering, and risk management, we could create trading tools that rival those used by major hedge funds.

Our Approach

We believe successful algorithmic trading requires three core elements:

  1. Rigorous Research: Every strategy is backtested across multiple market cycles and stress-tested against extreme scenarios. We use advanced statistical methods to ensure robustness.
  2. Robust Technology: Our infrastructure is built to institutional standards with redundancy, low-latency execution, and real-time monitoring.
  3. Risk Management: Capital preservation is paramount - every algorithm has multiple layers of risk controls including position limits, drawdown protection, and volatility filters.
