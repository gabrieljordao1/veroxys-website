Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2025

1. Introduction

Veroxys LLC (“we,” “us,” or “our”) values your privacy. This policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your personal data when you visit our website or purchase our Expert Advisors.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Account Data: Name, email, billing address, phone number when you sign up.
  • Payment Data: Processed securely by Stripe or PayPal—no card data stored on our servers.
  • Support Communications: Emails, chat transcripts, logs, screenshots you provide.

2.2 Technical & Usage Data

  • Log Data: IP address, browser type and version, pages visited, timestamps.
  • Cookies & Tracking: Session cookies for login, persistent cookies for preferences, Google Analytics (anonymized).

3. How We Use Your Information

  • Process purchases and deliver license keys.
  • Provide updates, bug fixes, and technical support.
  • Analyze website usage to improve features and performance.
  • Comply with legal obligations and enforce our agreements.

4. Data Sharing & Disclosure

We do not sell or rent personal data. We may share with:

  • Payment Processors: Stripe, PayPal for transaction fulfillment.
  • Service Providers: AWS, Cloudflare for hosting and delivery.
  • Legal Authorities: If required by law or to protect our rights.

5. Cookies & Similar Technologies

Cookies help keep you logged in, remember preferences, and track site performance. You can disable cookies via browser settings—but key functionality may break.

6. Your Rights

  • Access & Portability: Request a copy of your data.
  • Correction: Update inaccurate or incomplete information.
  • Deletion: Request erasure of your personal data, subject to legal requirements.
  • Opt-Out: Unsubscribe from marketing emails via the link in any email.

Submit requests to privacy@veroxys.com.

7. Data Security

We implement TLS encryption, secure key management, firewalls, and regular vulnerability scans. However, no system is completely immune to attack.

8. Children’s Privacy

Our products and services are not directed to children under 18. We do not knowingly collect data from minors.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Changes take effect when posted, and we’ll update the “Last updated” date.

10. Contact Us

Questions or concerns? Email us at privacy@veroxys.com.
