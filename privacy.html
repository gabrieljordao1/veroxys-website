Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2025

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: Name, email, billing address when you purchase an EA.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our website (pages visited, buttons clicked).
  • Support Requests: Any messages, logs or screenshots you provide to our support team.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Process purchases and deliver license keys.
  • Provide technical support and product updates.
  • Improve our site and EAs through usage analytics.

3. Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal cookies for sessions and anonymized Google Analytics for performance tracking.

4. Data Sharing

We never sell personal data. We share only with:

  • Payment processors (Stripe/PayPal).
  • Hosting providers (AWS, Cloudflare).

5. Your Rights

Request data access, correction, or deletion via privacy@veroxys.com.

6. Security

We use TLS encryption, secure key management, and conduct regular security audits.
