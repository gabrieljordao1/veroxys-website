Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 26, 2025

1. Information We Collect

  • Account Data: Name, email, billing address when you purchase an EA.
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our website (pages visited, buttons clicked).
  • Support Requests: Any messages, logs or screenshots you provide to our support team.

2. How We Use Your Data

  • Process purchases and deliver license keys.
  • Provide technical support and product updates.
  • Improve our site, EAs and documentation through usage analytics.

3. Cookies & Tracking

We use minimal cookies for session management and Google Analytics (anonymized) to track site performance.

4. Data Sharing & Third Parties

We never sell personal data. We may share with:

  • Payment processors (Stripe/PayPal) to fulfill purchases.
  • Hosting providers (AWS, Cloudflare) for site operations.

5. Your Rights

You may request data access, correction or deletion by emailing privacy@veroxys.com.

6. Security

We implement industry-standard encryption (TLS), secure key management, and regular security audits.
