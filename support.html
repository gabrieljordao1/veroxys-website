Support

Available Monday–Friday, 9 AM–5 PM Eastern Time.

1. Contact Methods

  • Email: support@veroxys.com
  • Live Chat: Click the chat widget in the bottom-right corner of the site.
  • Phone: +1 (800) VER-OXYZ (837-6999), 9 AM–5 PM ET.

2. Knowledge Base

Find in-depth guides, FAQs and troubleshooting at docs.veroxys.com.

3. Submitting a Support Ticket

  1. Log into your Veroxys account portal.
  2. Navigate to Support → Submit Ticket.
  3. Attach your EA license key, platform logs, and a detailed description.
  4. Expect a response within 24 hours on business days.

4. System Status & SLA

Monitor our license server & download portal status at status.veroxys.com. Critical issues are escalated with a target response time of 1 hour.
